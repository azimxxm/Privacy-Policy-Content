Privacy Policy for VeoTV Player

Last Updated: January 2025

INTRODUCTION
VeoTV Player ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our IPTV streaming application.

INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT
VeoTV Player does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data. We do not:
• Track your usage or viewing history
• Collect personal identification information
• Use analytics or tracking services
• Share data with third parties
• Display advertisements or use ad networks
• Require account creation or sign-up

DATA STORAGE
All data created within VeoTV Player is stored locally on your device:
• IPTV playlist URLs and channel data
• Favorite channels
• App preferences (theme, language, icon)
• Custom stream URLs

This data never leaves your device and is not backed up to our servers (we don't have any servers). If you delete the app, all local data is permanently removed.

NETWORK USAGE
VeoTV Player connects to the internet only to:
• Fetch IPTV playlists from URLs you provide
• Stream video content from sources you specify
• Load channel logos from playlist metadata

We do not monitor, log, or analyze these connections.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
VeoTV Player does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools. The app operates entirely independently.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
VeoTV Player does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

YOUR CONTENT
You are responsible for the IPTV playlists and streams you add to VeoTV Player. We do not provide, suggest, or distribute any content. Ensure you have legal rights to access any content you stream.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be posted within the app and on the App Store.
