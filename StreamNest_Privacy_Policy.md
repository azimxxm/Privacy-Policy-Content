# Privacy Policy for StreamNest

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

## Overview
StreamNest ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS app.

## What We Collect

### Information We DO NOT Collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Advertising identifiers
- User behavior analytics
- Browsing history (stored locally on your device only)
- Any form of tracking cookies

### Information Stored Locally:
StreamNest stores the following information ONLY on your device:
- Browsing history (URLs visited)
- Theme preference (dark/light mode)
- Language selection
- App settings

**This information is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.**

## How We Use Information

We do not use, sell, or share any user information. All data remains on your device.

## Data Security

Your information is protected by:
- Local device encryption (handled by iOS)
- No cloud synchronization
- No third-party servers
- No data transmission

## Children's Privacy

StreamNest is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Third-Party Services

StreamNest uses the following frameworks (read-only access):
- WebKit: For web browsing functionality
- AVFoundation: For audio playback
- UserDefaults: For local storage

None of these frameworks transmit your data.

## Web Content

When you browse websites through StreamNest, your information is subject to those websites' privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app.

## Contact Us

For privacy concerns or questions:
- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/azimxxm/StreamNest/issues
- Email: azimxxm@gmail.com

---

StreamNest is designed with privacy as the core principle. Your data belongs to you, always.
